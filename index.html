Story of the Galactic Empire

by Akademio

The Galactic Empire:

The Galactic Empire was the government that rose to power at the end of the Clone Wars.
Emerged from the democratic galactic republic into an authocracy.
With the emperor Palpatine as its leader.
The imperial military was a strong reminder of the power that the emperor wielded
An example of this is the construction of the Death Star. Its whole purpose was to control the galaxy under the threat of planet destruction.
Who built the original Death Star?
The wookies were enslaved by the Empire after an invasion to their home planet. Most of them ended up working at the construction of the Death Star.
